Bad Brain (Single Panel)

My name's Izzy and I'm a non-binary lesbian, an activist, and an artist. I have a Bachelor's degree in Psychology with a focus on queer experience, and my long-term goals involve not just conducting much needed empirical research on queer experiences, but also consistently remaining engaged with my local LGBT+ community and advocacy.

I use my art to reflect on my life as a queer person and create representations of queer people for queer people, ones that don't rely on cliches or narratives with underlying agendas but that portray us as we are, without concern for a straight and cis audience. This project, The Bad Homosexual, is the result of that mission.
